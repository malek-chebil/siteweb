### **Risk 1: Direct Link Through Blockchain** ‚ö†Ô∏è

**The Problem:**
1. **Binance knows your identity** (KYC - you had to verify)
2. **Bitcoin blockchain is public** - all transactions visible forever
3. **Blockchain analysis** can trace: Binance ‚Üí Your address ‚Üí Njalla
4. **If someone accesses Binance data** + blockchain, they can link:
   - Your real identity (Binance) 
   - To Njalla payment
   - Potentially to your domain

**How It Works:**
- Binance records show: "User X sent 0.001 BTC to address ABC..."
- Blockchain shows: "Address ABC sent 0.001 BTC to Njalla address XYZ..."
- Analysis tools can connect these dots

### **Risk 2: Njalla Account Linking** ‚ö†Ô∏è

**If discovered:**
- Law enforcement could request Binance records
- Match blockchain transactions
- Link your identity to Njalla account
- Link to your domain registration

### **Risk 3: Timing Analysis** ‚ö†Ô∏è
