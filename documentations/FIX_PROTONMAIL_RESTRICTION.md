### **Option 1: Add Recovery Email/Phone (Recommended)** ‚≠ê

**This is the easiest way to lift the restriction.**

#### Step 1: Add Recovery Email

1. **Login to ProtonMail**: https://mail.proton.me
2. **Go to Settings**:
   - Click your profile icon (top right)
   - Click "Settings" or "All settings"
3. **Go to Security**:
   - Click "Security" in left menu
   - Scroll to "Recovery methods"
4. **Add Recovery Email**:
   - Click "Add recovery email"
   - Enter a **different email address** (can be Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
   - Click "Send verification email"
   - Check that email inbox
   - Click verification link
   - Return to ProtonMail
5. **Verify Recovery Email**:
   - Should show "Verified" next to recovery email
6. **Try Njalla again**:
   - The restriction should be lifted
   - Try registering on Njalla again

#### Step 2: Add Phone Number (Alternative)

If you prefer phone verification:

1. **Go to Settings ‚Üí Security**
2. **Add Phone Number**:
   - Click "Add phone number"
   - Enter your phone number
   - Choose country code
   - Click "Send verification code"
   - Enter code received via SMS
3. **Verify Phone**:
   - Should show "Verified"
4. **Try Njalla again**

**‚ö†Ô∏è Privacy Note**: Adding phone number is less private, but recovery email is fine for anonymity.
