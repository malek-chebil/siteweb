### **Step 1: Monero Wallet (for Domain Registration)** ‚è±Ô∏è 15 minutes

**Why Monero?**

**Option A: Cake Wallet (Mobile - Easiest)** ‚≠ê Recommended

1. **Download Cake Wallet**:
   - iOS: App Store ‚Üí Search "Cake Wallet"
   - Android: Google Play Store ‚Üí Search "Cake Wallet"
   - **Or** F-Droid (more privacy-focused)

2. **Create New Wallet**:
   - Open app ‚Üí "Create New Wallet"
   - Choose wallet name: `DomainWallet` (or any name)
   - Set strong password (save it!)
   - **IMPORTANT**: Write down the **25-word seed phrase** on paper
   - Store seed phrase safely (this is your backup!)
   - Verify seed phrase when prompted

3. **Get Monero**:
   - You need ~‚Ç¨15-30 worth of Monero (for domain registration)
   - Option 1: Buy from exchange (Kraken, Binance, etc.) then withdraw to Cake Wallet
   - Option 2: Use exchange with Monero support

**Option B: Monero GUI (Desktop - More Advanced)**

1. **Download**: https://www.getmonero.org/downloads/
2. **Install** and create wallet
3. **Write down seed phrase** (25 words)
4. **Sync blockchain** (can take hours - use remote node for faster setup)

### **Step 2: Bitcoin Wallet (for Hosting Payment)** ‚è±Ô∏è 10 minutes

**Why Bitcoin?**
- 1984 Hosting accepts Bitcoin
- More widely accepted than Monero

**Electrum Wallet (Desktop - Recommended)**

1. **Download Electrum**:
   - Go to: https://electrum.org
   - Download for Windows (or your OS)
   - **Verify**: Check GPG signature if possible (security best practice)

2. **Install Electrum**:
   - Run installer
   - Launch Electrum

3. **Create New Wallet**:
   - Click "Create a new wallet"
   - Choose wallet name: `HostingWallet`
   - Choose "Standard wallet"
   - Choose "Create a new seed"
   - Choose "Segwit" (recommended - lower fees)
   - **IMPORTANT**: Write down the **12-word seed phrase** on paper
   - Store seed phrase safely
   - Verify seed phrase when prompted
   - Set strong wallet password

4. **Get Bitcoin**:
   - You need ~‚Ç¨10-20 worth of Bitcoin (for 1 month hosting)
   - Option 1: Buy from Binance/Kraken, then withdraw to Electrum
   - Option 2: Use any exchange with Bitcoin support
   - **Send Bitcoin** to your Electrum wallet address
